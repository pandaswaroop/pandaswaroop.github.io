Artificial Intelligence

Within AI, I am currently looking at,

  1. Investigating LLMs - Recent discourses suggest that large language models (LLMs) may be autonomous agents. Thus, it is important to examine the nature of agency that these models possess. Such agency can manifest in various ways such as moral reasoning, creativity, political orientation, cultural awareness, decision-making capabilities and other abilities.
  2. LLMs-Human Interaction - With LLMs gaining multimodal capabilities, innovative methods of user interaction with LLMs—beyond traditional prompting—are likely to emerge. Thus exploring and developing novel approaches for enhancing user engagement with LLMs and other AI systems becomes increasingly important.
