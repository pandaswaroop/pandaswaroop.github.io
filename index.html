Research Interests

I build solutions at the intersection of HCI and AI that organizations can apply where it matters most - whether in customer-facing products, employee tools, or executive decision-making. My focus: translating how humans interact with technology into outcomes organizations care about - adoption, trust, efficiency, and strategic advantage. From early-stage prototypes to deployments, I move from thought experiments to real execution - combining academic research rigor with practical strategies. The goal is to thoughtfully develop technology that delivers measurable value, stays human-centered, scales with the business, and advances the organization's strategic aims.
