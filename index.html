Research & Development Interests

My research & development interests broadly revolve around three themes,

  1. Human Computer Interaction - Within HCI, I am interested in developing formal, computational models that capture the dynamics of interaction between humans & (artificially and naturally intelligent) computers.
  2. Visualization - Within Viz, my interests lie in the development of models, frameworks, and grammars for the design of visual representations across different fields.
